Teacher Profile

Abhinav Sharma

Assistant Professor, Department of Computer Applications, Government College Bhoranj (Tarkwari), Hamirpur, Himachal Pradesh.

Academic Profile

Abhinav Sharma is a cybersecurity researcher and educator with doctoral research focused on enhancing SIEM capabilities through threat analytics and intelligence.

  • PhD, Cyber Security - Himachal Pradesh University, Shimla
  • M.Tech, Computer Science - Himachal Pradesh University, Shimla
  • B.Tech, Information Technology - UIIT, HPU Shimla
  • Advanced Program in Cyber Security and Cyber Defense - IIT Kanpur

Teaching Areas

Computer applications, computer fundamentals, office productivity tools, cybersecurity foundations, and practical computer skills.

Research Interests

SIEM, threat analytics, SOC automation, threat intelligence, incident response, agentic AI, multi-agent systems, LLMs for security, explainable AI, alert triage, and insider-threat detection.

Experience

Assistant Professor at Government College Bhoranj since 2018. Earlier roles include Programmer at Himachal Pradesh Administrative Tribunal, Assistant Programmer at Hon'ble High Court of Himachal Pradesh, and State Project Coordinator for MDMS-MIS and ARMS.
