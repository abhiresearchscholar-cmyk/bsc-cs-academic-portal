2

3

Read lecture notes before class and download slides or notes when available.

4

Practice Regularly

Complete assignments before due dates, attempt quizzes for revision, and check announcements regularly.

Important Instructions

  • Bring a notebook to class and write important terms in simple language.
  • Practice lab activities repeatedly until you can do them independently.
  • Submit assignments before the due date through the given link.
  • Use the AI Course Assistant only for learning support, not for copying answers.

How Quizzes and Assignments Work

Practice quizzes help you revise topics. Quiz marks are not officially recorded unless the teacher announces it separately.

Assignments may be submitted through Google Classroom, Google Form, or another method announced by the teacher.

The AI Assistant is for explanation and revision only. It works only in the computer lab / college LAN when the local LLM backend is running.

AI Learning Support

The AI Assistant can explain concepts, give simple examples, help revise lecture topics, and support English, Hindi, or Hinglish prompts.

It should not be used for copying assignment answers or exam answers.
